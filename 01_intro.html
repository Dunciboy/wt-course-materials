Our coding philosophy (1)

  • stick to web standards
  • strict separation of content, presentation and behaviour
  • well-documented, lean and fast code
  • modern, responsive, accessible, userfriendly
  • → standards-compliance
  • → why?
The web and customer expectations changes contantly. What works today, probably won't be acceptable tomorrow. To give an impression of how much the web changed, visit the space jam website, still unaltered since 1996.
